The previous steps showed you how to Create a Fixlist, Add photos of what to be fixed to the active fixlist, add participants, whom you trust to fix the issue on the photo or with whom you want to discuss it.
Now you are going to discuss a photo with participants. For example, something needs to be clarified. To start a conversation on the subject with participants, follow the below steps:

  • On the Home screen, tap the photo you want to discuss

  • The photo chat opens:

  • Enter you message at the bottom of the screen in the input field.
    You can attach photos to messages - they will be displayed along with the message in the chat.

  • Send your message

  • People invited to the photo discussion or a fixlist will be able to chat with you on the subject here.

Zooming photo

You can zoom both a photo in a fixlist and a photo attached in the chat.

Tap the button to zoom a fixlist photo, or tap the photo itself to zoom an attached photo in the chat:

When everything is fixed according to the photo, you might want to mark the issue as fixed. See the Mark Photo as Fixed article to learn how to do this.

But before a photo is resolved / fixed, have a look at the Inspo section. You will definitely find some materials, furniture, equipment, etc., relevant to the fixlist topic / object.
