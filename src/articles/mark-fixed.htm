When everything is done with a particular issue, all fixes are made, in order to distinguish open and fixed issue, you might want to mark the issue as fixed somehow.

For this, every item in a fixlist has a special tick :

To mark an item as fixed, tap this tick. It becomes filled with green:

Now, on the Home screen, you won't see this item within the active fixlist. Instead, it will be displayed on the Archive tab of the Home screen:

So you can manage items in fixlists and keep your fixing history.

If an issue needs to be improved, re-fixed, etc., you can tap the tick again in the Archive, so that item appears back in the Active list.

Delete an item

As an alternative, you can delete items from fixlists. For this, from the Active tab of the current fixlist, tap the button under the item you want to delete:

In the appeared menu, select Delete:

The item is deleted from the fixlist now, as well as all the information related to it: chat, fixing history, Inspo attachments, etc.

It is highly recommended not to delete items from fixlists. They won't bother you from the archive if you just mark them as fixed.
